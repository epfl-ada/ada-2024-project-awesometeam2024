What is Success ?

For this story, we're looking at box office revenue as our measure of success. But money isn't everything. A movie can be loved by everyone who watched it or win tons of awards even if it didn't make a ton of money. By sticking with box office revenue, we're focusing on financial success, but it's important to keep in mind there's more than one way to measure a movie's impact.

Timing Is Everything

As we can imagine, the release date as a strong impact on the film's performance. First by plotting the data, we discover that summer is the best period to release a movie. Too short, too long, or just right?

The runtime of a movie is a key factor in its success. Too short and the audience might feel cheated, too long and they might get bored. But before that, the evloution of the runtime of movies reflects the technical evolution of the industry. In the early days, movies were very short. But when technique was no longer a problem, movies started to get longer and longer before shrinking again to stabillize around 105 minutes.

Dual interactive plot with selector for the year : 1 plot for average revenue, 1 plot for average runtime

Of course, you could say that this data does not represent he ground truth but only shows what happened in the past. And you are absolutly right ! We are not saying that short movies will performed less but simply that less and less movies are produced with a short runtime. Who Brings the Magic?

The Youth Advantage vs. Timeless Talent

In general, films with younger casts seem to perform better on average, suggesting a potential trend that producers might want to explore further. Younger actors may connect better with certain target audiences, particularly with the youngest. However, while this observation is intriguing, it's important to approach it with caution. The smaller sample size for younger casts makes the data less stable and more prone to volatility, meaning this trend could be more coincidental than reliable. In contrast, the larger quantity of samples for films featuring adult casts provides more robust statitics. It's a good place to remind you to be careful when reading data !
